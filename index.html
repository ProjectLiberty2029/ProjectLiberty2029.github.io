"Project 2029" is shorthand for the work that must begin now, and continue forever: to replace tyranny and authoritarianism with Liberty and Justice for All.
We hope you will join this collaboration to provide an aggressive, yet public, road map for restoring Liberty and Justice and turning the United States back from the calamities of the current administration.
